  16. Read the following paragraph and answer the question below.
    Emma is a talented artist. She loves to paint landscapes and portraits. Last month, her paintings were displayed at a local art gallery. Many people attended the exhibition and praised her work. Emma was thrilled to see her art appreciated by so many.
    What is Emma's passion?
  17. Read the following paragraph and answer the question below.
    Daniel is a dedicated scientist. He conducts research in the field of renewable energy. Last week, he presented his findings at an international conference, where his work was praised for its potential to address energy challenges. Daniel's commitment to advancing sustainable solutions is evident in his contributions to scientific knowledge.
    What is Daniel's area of expertise?
  18. Read the following paragraph and answer the question below.
    Alex is planning a trip to a foreign country. He has been studying the language and culture of that country for months. He wants to make the most of his visit and connect with the locals. Alex has even learned some basic phrases to communicate with the people there.
    What is Alex preparing for?
  19. Read the following paragraph and answer the question below.
    Sarah enjoys reading books of all genres. She often loses herself in the stories and characters. Yesterday, she finished reading a mystery novel that kept her guessing until the very end. Sarah loves how books allow her to explore new worlds and perspectives.
    What is Sarah's favorite activity?
  20. Read the following paragraph and answer the question below.
    David is a dedicated environmentalist. He volunteers for local clean-up events and promotes eco-friendly practices in his community. Last week, he organized a tree-planting activity in a nearby park. David believes that small actions can make a big difference in protecting the planet.
    What does David care about deeply?
  21. Read the following paragraph and answer the question below.

    What instrument does Sophie play?
